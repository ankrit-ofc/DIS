Before — current screen

Screenshot from device · Blue-heavy, identical cards, placeholder icons.

7 things to fix
1
Massive blue header — eats the top third of the screen. Shrink to ~120px so the catalogue breathes.
2
Grey cube placeholders on every card — reads as "missing image". Replace with solid brand-color tiles showing brand name typed.
3
Global "13%" badge on every product — a discount on everything is not a discount, it's noise. Only show badges for real deals.
4
Full-width blue Add buttons — too much blue, every row looks the same. Shrink to a small + square in the card corner.
5
Cartoon emoji categories (🍺 ⚡ 🥃) — feels like a kids' app, not a B2B wholesale tool. Use typed chips.
6
No shop identity beyond "Demo Store". No credit balance, no "order again", no context.
7
Announcement card takes space but says "Welcome to DISTRO…" — marketing fluff. Either remove or use for real info (today's deal, delivery cutoff).

After — target design

Warm B2B distributor feel. Real brand colors. Credit front-and-center.
